One Punch Man

One Punch Man is a Japanese manga and anime series created by ONE and illustrated by Yusuke Murata. It follows Saitama, an ordinary man who becomes a hero for fun and trains so intensely that he gains the power to defeat any opponent with a single punch.Though unmatched in strength, Saitama struggles with boredom and a lack of fulfillment, as no battle challenges him anymore. Alongside his cyborg disciple Genos and other heroes from the Hero Association, he faces monstrous villains and chaotic threats to humanity.The series blends action, comedy, and satire, humorously exploring what it means to be a hero and the emptiness of absolute power
