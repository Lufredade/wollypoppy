Sakamoto Days

Sakamoto Days is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Yuto Suzuki. It follows Taro Sakamoto, a legendary hitman who was once feared as the world’s greatest assassin. After falling in love and starting a family, Sakamoto retires from his violent past to live a peaceful life running a small convenience store.However, his quiet days don’t last long—when old enemies and organizations from the underworld come after him, Sakamoto must rely on his incredible skills, sharp instincts, and newfound allies to protect his loved ones, all while maintaining his peaceful lifestyle.Blending action, comedy, and heart, Sakamoto Days delivers fast-paced fights, creative assassins, and a wholesome yet hilarious story about balancing everyday life with a not-so-ordinary past.
