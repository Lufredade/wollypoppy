Demon Slayer

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba is a Japanese manga and anime series created by Koyoharu Gotouge. The story follows Tanjiro Kamado, a kind-hearted boy whose family is slaughtered by demons. His younger sister, Nezuko, survives but is transformed into a demon herself. Determined to find a cure for her and avenge his family, Tanjiro joins the Demon Slayer Corps, a group of skilled warriors dedicated to hunting demons and protecting humanity.Alongside his friends Zenitsu and Inosuke, Tanjiro faces powerful demons and uncovers the secrets behind their existence, all while striving to defeat the demon king Muzan Kibutsuji. The series is celebrated for its emotional storytelling, breathtaking animation, and themes of family, perseverance, and compassion.
